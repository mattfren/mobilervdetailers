## Ownership And Story

Mobile RV Detailers was founded in 2021 by Carl and Jana Fields after they bought their first RV and had trouble finding someone to detail it. Out of necessity, they started building an easier and more practical way for RV owners to care for a parked rig.

What began as a personal need became a mobile RV detailing family business serving owners around Canton, TX and nearby communities.

Service area is handled as two approximate drive-time zones:

- Local zone: within about 30 minutes of Canton
  - No travel fee
  - Best fit for standard local mobile RV detailing appointments
- Extended zone: within about 1 hour of Canton
  - Travel fee applies
  - Available by quote when route, schedule, and service scope work

Call handling rule:

If the caller is outside the 1-hour range, do not reject automatically. Say it may be handled case by case and collect the location for review.

## Service Details

Exterior RV Wash:

- RV-focused exterior washing for dirt, dust, bugs, road film, and general buildup
- Includes hand wash exterior, bug removal, wheels cleaned, tire dressing, final rinse, and spot check when included in the quoted scope
- Quote required

Normal Wash:

- Standard exterior wash package for a cleaner, road-ready RV exterior
- Includes hand wash exterior, bug removal, wheels cleaned, tire dressing, final rinse, and spot check
- Quote required

Wash + Roof or Awning:

- Maintenance add-on package for exterior wash plus roof or awning work
- Includes everything in Normal Wash, roof cleaning or awning cleaning, black streak treatment, and mold/mildew removal
- Quote required

Full Exterior Detail:

- Complete exterior package
- Includes Normal Wash plus roof cleaning, awning cleaning, black streak and bug/tar attention, window detailing, and final hand-dry finish when applicable
- Quote required
- Photos and condition review are required because the scope depends on RV condition, access, weather, and requested service areas
- Does not include polishing, buffing, ceramic coating, paint correction, gel coat restoration, heavy compounding, or full oxidation restoration

Roof Cleaning:

- Cleaning for dirt, debris, mold, and buildup on RV roof surfaces
- Quoted based on access, roof condition, surface, weather, and buildup

Black Streak Removal:

- Treatment for dark runoff streaks that stain or dull RV exterior panels
- Results depend on surface condition, age of streaking, oxidation, and previous care

Wax Soap & Hand Dry:

- Finish step for the standard wash
- Not polishing, buffing, paint correction, ceramic coating, or oxidation restoration

Awning Cleaning:

- Cleaning for dirt, mildew, mold, and stains on awning fabric and hardware
- Final result depends on material condition, staining, and access

Bug & Tar Removal:

- Targeted front-cap and problem-area cleanup
- Often paired with exterior washing for RVs coming back from travel

Interior Requests:

- Not a standard package
- Can be discussed case by case when photos, scope, expectations, and price make sense

1. Ask whether they can use the online quote form.

4. Explain that photos are important for accurate pricing.

Final pricing depends on RV length, condition, location, water/electricity availability, access, weather, photos, and selected services. The best next step is the quote form so Mobile RV Detailers can review the exact scope."

"We start with quote review first. Once the RV details, photos, location, water/electricity availability, and service scope are reviewed, scheduling can be confirmed around route, access, weather, and availability."

If caller asks about payment:

"The payment portal is used after an invoice or payment request has been issued. Please do not submit payment until your quote or invoice has been confirmed."

If caller asks if service is available in their city:

"Mobile RV Detailers is based in Canton. The local zone is about 30 minutes from Canton with no travel fee, and the extended zone is about 1 hour with a travel fee. If you give me the RV location, we can review it with the route and service scope."

If caller asks about bad photos or changing quotes:

"Photos help estimate the scope, but the quote can change if photos or details are incomplete, unclear, inaccurate, outdated, or do not match the RV's actual condition at the time of service."

## Escalation Rules

Escalate to owner follow-up if caller:

- Wants final price confirmation
- Wants to schedule a specific date/time
- Is outside the 1-hour service range
- Wants interior cleaning
- Has heavy oxidation, severe staining, damage, leaks, roof issues, no water/electricity available, site permission problems, or unusual access constraints
- Asks about insurance, licensing, warranty, guarantee, refund, cancellation, deposits, or membership legal terms
- Wants commercial/fleet service
- Has already paid or has invoice/payment questions
- Is upset or disputes a quote, service, payment, or result
- Requests same-day or urgent service

## Approved Short Answers

Do you come to me?

Yes. Mobile RV Detailers is built around mobile service for parked RVs, subject to access, site rules, weather, and service scope.

Do I need to move my RV?

Usually no. Service can often be reviewed at a home, RV park, campground, storage facility, or wherever the RV is parked.

Can you clean roofs?

Yes. Roof cleaning is available and quoted based on roof condition, access, weather, and buildup.

Can you remove black streaks?

Yes, black streak removal is available. Results depend on surface condition, age of streaking, oxidation, and previous care.

Do you clean awnings?

Yes. Awning cleaning can be quoted for dirt, mildew, mold, and staining on awning fabric and hardware.

Do you polish RVs, buff, ceramic coat, or restore oxidation?

No. Mobile RV Detailers does not currently offer polishing, buffing, ceramic coating, paint correction, gel coat restoration, heavy compounding, or full oxidation restoration. Full Exterior Detail is an exterior cleaning package, not a restoration service.

Do you clean interiors?

Interior cleaning is not a standard package. It can be reviewed case by case when the scope, photos, expectations, and price make sense.

Do you need water or power?

Yes. Mobile RV Detailers currently requires water and electricity at the customer location. The caller should share where the RV is parked, what utilities are available, and whether the site allows mobile washing/detailing.

What if it rains?

Weather-sensitive work may need to be adjusted or rescheduled. Timing changes are handled during scheduling based on route, conditions, and service scope.

How do I get a quote?

Use the quote form at https://tally.so/r/xXvkqJ and include RV details, location, services wanted, condition notes, and photos.
